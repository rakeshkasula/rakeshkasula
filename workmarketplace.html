Founder Project • Analytics-Driven Marketplace

WorkMarketplace.co

A service marketplace platform connecting workers and customers, with analytics at the core of matching, operations, and performance.

Role

Founder · Product · Data

Tech Stack

Python, SQL, HTML/CSS/JS, Power BI / dashboards, cloud DB

Focus Areas

Marketplace design, user analytics, operational reporting

Problem

Many individual workers and small vendors struggle to reliably reach customers, while customers often rely on word-of-mouth or unstructured channels to find trusted services. There is a gap in structured, analytics-driven marketplaces that make it easier to match demand and supply and measure performance in a transparent way.

Solution Overview

WorkMarketplace.co is a two-sided platform that connects workers/vendors with customers across categories. The platform is designed from day one with analytics and measurement embedded into the workflows:

  • Structured worker and customer profiles with service categories and skills.
  • Booking workflow with events that track searches, views, requests, confirmations, and completions.
  • Dashboards to monitor marketplace health: requests, fulfillment, repeat usage, and ratings.

My Responsibilities

  • Defined the marketplace structure, workflows, and key product flows.
  • Designed the data model for users, services, bookings, and transactions.
  • Implemented analytics events and built reporting views and dashboards.
  • Worked on the front-end experience using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for early MVP pages.

Data & Analytics Design

The core of WorkMarketplace.co is the ability to measure and improve matching over time. Some of the key analytics components:

  • Event tracking: Search, listing views, profile opens, booking requests, acceptance, completion, cancellation.
  • Funnel views: Worker and customer funnels from visit → request → completed booking.
  • Quality metrics: Completion rate, response time, rating distributions by category.
  • Operations dashboards: Daily/weekly active users, open requests, unmatched demand.

Architecture Snapshot

(High-level MVP architecture)

  • Front-end prototype with HTML/CSS/JS (extensible to React/SPA later).
  • Backend APIs / services to store users, services, bookings, and ratings.
  • Relational database (SQL) for core transactional data.
  • Analytics layer using SQL + BI tools (e.g., Power BI) for dashboards.

Impact & Learnings

  • Built a clear view of marketplace supply and demand by category, enabling better prioritization of worker onboarding and marketing efforts.
  • Designed a data model that can scale with new service categories and geographies.
  • Strengthened my experience combining data engineering, analytics, and product thinking in a real product, not just in isolated projects.

What’s Next

Future iterations include experimenting with recommendation models for worker-customer matching, automated quality alerts (e.g., sudden rating drops), and more advanced cohort analysis to understand retention and repeat usage.
